Use a table (in Excel, CSV, Word, or PDF) with these exact column headers in the first row:

ColumnDescriptionExample
test_titleGroups questions into one testConstitutional Law
question_textThe question itselfWhat is the supreme law?
option_aAnswer option AThe President
option_bAnswer option BThe Constitution
option_cAnswer option CThe Courts
option_dAnswer option DParliament
correct_answerCorrect letter: A, B, C or DB
💡 Questions with the same test_title are grouped into one test automatically.

Write questions in plain text inside your .docx or .pdf — no table needed. Use this structure:

Test: Constitutional Law 1. What is the supreme law of Uzbekistan? A) Presidential decree B) The Constitution C) Parliamentary law D) International treaties Answer: B 2. When was the Constitution adopted? A) 1991 B) 1993 C) 1992 D) 1995 Answer: C Test: Civil Law 1. What does "plaintiff" mean? A) The judge B) The defendant C) The person who files a lawsuit D) The lawyer Answer: C
💡 Rules:
  • Start a new test with Test: Title here
  • Number questions: 1. or 1) or Q1:
  • Options: A) or A. or A:
  • Mark the answer: Answer: B or Correct: B
  • Works with both .docx and .pdf (text-based, not scanned)
